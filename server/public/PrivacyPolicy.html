Tracking Technology ("Cookies" and Related Technologies)


Instanews uses tracking technology (“cookies”) on the Website and in the Application and by using the Website or Application you agree to their use. The Website and Services use “session cookies,” which improve your user experience by storing certain information from your current visit on your computer or Device.


Specifically, we use the following cookies to perform the following functions:


  • Session cookie for the Services to identify the Logged-in User. It expires at the closing of the session;

  • Authentication cookie for the Services on the Website to facilitate signing in;

  • Google Analytics cookie to provide general internal and user analytics and to assist in identifying possible fraudulent activities;

  • For support services, we use a breadcrumb to help us analyse and diagnose reported problems.


Your browser or Device can be set to refuse cookies or delete them after they have been stored. Please refer to your browser or Device’s help function for instructions. Please note that deleting or refusing these cookies may reduce your user experience by requiring you to re-enter certain information, including information required to use the Services. Furthermore, as we use authentication cookies, deleting those cookies may prevent certain functions, including the entirety of the Services, from working at all. Instanews and its Representatives shall not be held responsible for any function that may not operate after you refuse or delete cookies, or any losses or damages (pecuniary or otherwise) arising from those functions not operating, or having reduced functionality.
